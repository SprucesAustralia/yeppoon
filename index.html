Your Role in the A-Team

Cleaner 1: Window & Finishing Specialist

Your first priority is all interior glass, including windows, tracks, frames, and seals. Once perfected, you'll assist with high-level dusting and the final wipe-down of all joinery and surfaces.

Cleaner 2: Floor Care Specialist

Your mission is the 460m² of carpet cleaning, followed by leading the vacuuming of all hard floors and assisting with baseboard cleaning.

Cleaner 3: Kitchen & Wet Areas Specialist

You will own the deep cleaning and sanitisation of all kitchens and bathrooms, polishing all fixtures and mirrors to a perfect shine.

Cleaner 4: Walls & Fixtures Specialist

Your mission is the top-down cleaning of all vertical surfaces, including walls, switch plates, joinery, cabinets, and built-in furniture.

Teamwork makes the dream work! Once primary tasks are complete, everyone will work together on the final mopping, scrubbing, and detailed floor finishing.
